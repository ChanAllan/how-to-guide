You might noticed that in the connection code there are 4 parameters: mysql:host=localhost, dbname=test, $username, and $password.

For mysql:host=localhost, you would need to replace localhost with your database host that you are wanting to access.

For dbname=test, you would need to replace test with your database name that you are wanting to access.

For $username and $password, these two parameters represent your username and password to access your database. However, the $username and $password are passed in from a separate file.
